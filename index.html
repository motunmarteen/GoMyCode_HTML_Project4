THE ORIGINAL

The tale begins with our namesake's profession:the 3Ms. Like all envoys, the original 3Ms' jobwas to be the manifestation of his homeland onforeign soils. But, he was always different.Regardless of where his work took him, he stoodout and became renowned for his refined taste foreverything vintage.

His suits were tailored to fit his mid 20thcentury style, his hair reflected a comb-over thatmany thought had been lost to another era, and hisdemeanour was class itself. He captivated crowdsand demanded crisp perfection. The 3Ms wastimeless.
